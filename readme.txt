Join your customers to groups according to their total sales.

== Description ==

Your customers will be joined to groups according to their total sales.
Apply discounts, get more access, or simply rewards the best customers.

= Features: =
  * According to the total sales, your customers will be added to a group

= Pro version: =
  * You can select more levels if you need it.

Official site: [eggemplo](http://eggemplo.com/plugins/woocommerce-groups-by-sales-light)
